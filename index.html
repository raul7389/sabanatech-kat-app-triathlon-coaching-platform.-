About Me

Your IRONMAN certified coach on the road to the finish line

Training Champions Since 2018

I am Katherine Gomez Aya, Ironman Certified Coach and triathlon training specialist. With over 6 years of experience preparing athletes of all levels, from beginners to Ironman competitors, my passion is helping you cross that finish line.

My approach combines sports training science with the mindset of a champion. I design personalized plans that integrate swimming, cycling, and athletics, optimizing each session to maximize your performance and minimize injury risk.

My mission is simple but powerful: to guide you to conquer your athletic goals, whether completing your first sprint triathlon or achieving a podium in an Ironman 140.6.
